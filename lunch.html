Lunch

Tuesdays, 12:00–1:00 p.m. — Zoom

During the school year, the HCI CoR meets for lunch each week. Students are welcome to sign up for a slot to describe their current progress, get feedback on early research results, or practice research talks. The first Tuesday of each month, we have a social lunch hour!


This term we will meet virtually, due to COVID-19. Please call in via the Zoom link in the HCI Lunch announcement email.
